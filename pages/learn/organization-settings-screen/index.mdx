The organization settings screen is where you can configure your organization information.
It can be accessed by clicking on <Icon><ChevronDownIcon/></Icon> icon next to your organization name, then selecting "Settings".

On this page, you can:

- Update your organization settings
- Delete your organization

<ProductVideo src={hero} />

## Update your organization settings

You can change your organization's name and logo under the "organization settings" section. The logo will be displayed on the [organization dashboard screen](/learn/organization-dashboard-screen) and it needs to be a JPG or PNG, at least 160px x 160px.

## Delete your organization

If you wish to delete your organization, you can do so under the "danger zone" section. Clicking the delete button will prompt a dialog with an extra confirmation step.
