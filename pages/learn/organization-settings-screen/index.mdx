The organization settings screen is where you can configure your organization information.
It can be accessed by clicking on <Icon><ChevronDownIcon/></Icon> icon next to your organization name, then selecting "Settings".

<ProductImage src={hero} />

## Update your organization settings

Over here you can change your organization's name and logo. The logo will be displayed on the [organization dashboard screen](/learn/organization-dashboard-screen) and it needs to be a JPG or PNG, at least 160px x 160px.

<ProductImage src={settings} />

## Delete your organization

If you wish to delete your organization, this is where you do it. Clicking the delete button will prompt an overlay with an extra confirmation step.
